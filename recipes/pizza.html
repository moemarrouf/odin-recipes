Ingredients

  • 6 ounces Italian sausage
  • 1 cup diced sweet peppers and onions
  • 1 1/2 cups prepared pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil, divided
  • Dough

Steps

  1. For dough, mix together flour, yeast, and water with a wooden spoon until almost all flour has disappeared and a rough dough is formed.
  2. Transfer dough into an oiled container with a lid, rub dough with a few drops of olive oil and cover. Refrigerate for 24 hours or until doubled in size.
  3. Remove dough from the refrigerator and let stand on the counter for 1 hour to come to room temperature.
  4. Drizzle a teaspoon of olive oil over dough and brush to coat evenly.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 15 minutes. Remove pizza crust to a cooling rack to cool to room temperature, about 15 minutes. Remove parchment from pan if used, then return crust to the pan.
  6. Spread crust with sauce, scatter over sausage and vegetables, sprinkle with mozzarella and Monterey Jack cheese, and finish with a dusting of Parmesan.
