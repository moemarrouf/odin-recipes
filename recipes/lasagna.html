Ingredients

  • ½ pound ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley, divided
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 3 large eggs
  • ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5 to 10 more minutes.
  5. Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
